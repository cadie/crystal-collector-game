Crystal Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystal cards. By clicking on a crystal card you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number displayed. If your total score goes above the random number, you lose.

The value of each crystal is hidden and can only be determined by determining how much each crystal is adding to you current score once you click on them.

Each time a new game starts, the random number will change and so will the values of the crystal cards.
